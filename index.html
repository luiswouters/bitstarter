FAQ

Will there be physical tickets?
No. Clients will receive a ticket with a number by e-mail, that he will have to show in the event. To valid the ticket, the business will have a desktop and a mobile app.
Will it be possible to sell assigned seats?
Yes. It will be possible to register seats by number or code together of seats map, where clients will be able to view it's location.
How is it going to be fraud control?
There will be a series of mechanisms to assure final clients, in case of fraud attempt, verifying sellers and not transferring payment to denounced events.A question which is commonly asked by users.
